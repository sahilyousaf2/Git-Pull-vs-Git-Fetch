About Our Church

We are a community of believers dedicated to spreading God's love and message

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope in our community for over 25 years. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of everyone who walks through our doors.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and make a difference. Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, you'll find a home here among friends who care about your spiritual journey.
